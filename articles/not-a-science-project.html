How to stop predictive maintenance becoming a science project

Successful PdM needs a commercial owner, a maintenance workflow, clear asset scope and a repeatable path from pilot to scale.

The common mistake

A pilot proves that analytics can find something interesting, but nobody defines how the business will use it repeatedly.

The technical reality

A prediction has limited value if it does not connect to a maintenance decision, work process, ownership model or measurable operational outcome.

The practical answer

Define the asset scope, decision process, value metric, data readiness and scale path before the pilot begins.

Workshop questions

Who acts on the insight? What decision changes? What is the cost of failure? What does success look like after 90 days? What would be needed to scale to the next site?
