OT to cloud: the uncomfortable middle of industrial digitalisation

The real project risk often sits between machine data, site networks, historians, edge devices and the people responsible for cybersecurity.

The common mistake

People often talk about AI and cloud platforms before confirming how machine data will actually move from the asset to the application.

The technical reality

Industrial environments include legacy PLCs, SCADA, historians, fieldbus systems, network segmentation, cybersecurity rules, missing tags and inconsistent naming conventions.

The practical answer

Map the architecture before promising the outcome. Clarify the data source, protocol, ownership, sample rate, quality, transformation needs and security boundary.

Workshop questions

Where does the signal live today? Who owns that system? Is the data timestamped and contextualised? Can it leave the site? Does it need edge processing? Who signs off the connection?
